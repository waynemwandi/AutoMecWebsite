F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do you offer a Collection & Delivery service?

    Yes, just let us know you want this service through out contact form, and one of our Service Advisors will contact you to confirm the collection and delivery details. Please Note: this service has a small charge.

  • How Often Should I Have My Brakes Checked?

    It is important to have your brakes checked often. Most folks have their brakes checked when they have their tires rotated. One of the mechanics at our service center can check the thickness of your brake pads to ensure that they are running at peak conditions. Your brake pads are essential for maintaining stopping power, so be sure to have them checked every chance that you get.

  • Do you repair all types of cars?

    Our complete automobile repair and maintenance service caters to vehicle repair and maintenance of for a number of manufacturers from Europe, America, Japan, Korea and Germany. For a detailed list of the makes we repair, please contact us

  • I can't get to your garage during the day What can I do?

    We are happy to arrange to pick up your vehicle either from work or home. Just let us know. We will also be happy to drop it back off when we’re done.

  • How often should I contact you for an oil change?

    This depends on the make and type of your vehicle. You can refer to your vehicle owner’s manual for guidance in this aspect. Alternately, you can share relevant details about your vehicle’s make, age and type of use with our team and they will guide you on the required frequency of oil change to keep your vehicle functioning smoothly.

  • My service light is on, will you be able to reset it?

    Yes. We have all the necessary electronic equipment to reset service lights and variable service indicators.
